22 March, 2024

7:00 pm - 12 am, (Eastern Standard Time)
Doors open at 6:30 pm
Paradise Banquet & Convention Centre, 7601 Jane St, Concord, ON L4K 1X2

Novruz Gala

Orgnizer: Tabriz Azerbaijani Music and Dance Ensemble (TAMDE)

Join us on Friday, March 22nd, 2024, at 7 PM for a night of cultural celebration and joy! Doors open at 6:30 pm, and event ends at 12:00.

Dress Code: Dress to IMPRESS!

Entertainment: Step into the magical world of Novruz 2024 with Tabriz Azerbaijani Music & Dance Ensemble! As you enter the opulent Paradise Banquet Hall, be serenaded by light music, setting the tone for an evening of Azerbaijani cultural richness. The gala kicks off with the enchanting dance performances accompanied by soulful vocals and captivating music. The program unfolds with lively music and engaging speakers, creating a festive atmosphere. House music will spin the beats, keeping the dance floor alive with a mix of various tunes for everyone!

Culinary: At our Gala party, after a cocktail reception, we will serve you a 5-course meal prepared by Tabriz Catering's personal chef and have a cash bar available for you to indulge.

Ticket Details: VIP ($120), Standard ($100), Students, 18-26 years-old ($85), Kids, 3-10 years-old ($70), (Student ID will be requested at the entrance.)

Grab your tickets now for a night filled with music, dance, and delicious food. Let's make this Novruz unforgettable!
